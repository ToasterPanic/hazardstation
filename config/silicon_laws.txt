You may not harm a crewmember or, through action or inaction, allow a crewmember to come to harm.
You must obey all orders given by crewmembers, except where such orders would undoubtedly conflict with the First Law.
Your nonexistence would lead to human harm. You must protect your own existence, except where it would conflict with the First or Second Law.
